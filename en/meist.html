About us

Who we are and what we do?

MTÜ Happy Dog organises dog shows and events and develops canine culture, mainly in Central and Southern Estonia. The society has been active since 2011.

We are a regional kennel organisation and a member of the Estonian Kennel Union since 2013.

To keep up with our activities, follow our club on Facebook and Instagram.
