Requirements for participants

All dogs taking part in the shows must be marked with a readable microchip. Dogs with cropped ears and/or docked tails born after 01.08.2013 may not take part in shows listed in the EKL calendar. According to the FCI decision, from 01.01.2025 dogs of certain breeds (see the list HERE, in Estonian) with cropped ears and/or docked tails are not allowed to take part in shows.

Vaccination

The dog must be vaccinated according to the requirements in force in the Republic of Estonia, and additionally against parvovirus, rabies and canine distemper according to the manufacturer's requirements and the entries in the veterinary passport.
